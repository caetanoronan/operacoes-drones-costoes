Usage Guide - Drone Field App

Quick guide to prepare, record, review and export rocky shore drone missions.

Before You Start

The app stores records in the browser using local storage. It can work in the field after loading, but you should export a backup at the end of the activity.

Recommended Workflow

  1. Open the app on the phone, tablet or notebook used in the field.
  2. Fill the Mission tab with project, team, location, date and objective.
  3. Review Pre-flight before powering or launching the drone.
  4. Record wind, rain, visibility, tide, light and risks in Conditions.
  5. Complete the Flight plan with altitude, area, speed, overlap and sensor.
  6. During or after the flight, fill Flight log with times, GPS, photos, videos, folder and occurrences.
  7. After downloading the data, complete Post-flight.
  8. Click Save mission and review the Data tab.
  9. At the end of the day, export CSV, Excel and JSON backup.

Supabase Sync

  1. In Supabase, run supabase_drones_schema.sql once in SQL Editor.
  2. Open the app and go to Cloud.
  3. Check the Supabase URL, publishable/anon key, campaign and device.
  4. Click Save and connect or Test connection.
  5. Use Upload local data to send records to the cloud.
  6. Use Download cloud data to recover or merge campaign records.

Use only the publishable or anon public key in the app. Never paste a service role key into public pages.

Export and Backup

  • CSV: recommended for spreadsheets and databases.
  • Excel: simple .xls file for quick review.
  • JSON: full backup to restore records in the app.
